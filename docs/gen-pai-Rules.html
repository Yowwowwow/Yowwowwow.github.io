gèn pái: How to Play

Game link: https://yowwowwow.github.io/gen-pai

Each player starts with 4 HP and 2 cards in hand.
At the start of each player's turns, they have to draw 2 cards, or 1 if they have 3 cards in hand, or 0 if they have 4 cards in hand.
Reduce the opponent's HP to 0 to win! It's a tie if both players' HP reach 0 simultaneously.
There are 15 cards in total.
There could be situations where both players have 4 cards in hand but can't play any. Although the game softlocks in this case, you can count it as a tie.
gèn (4): Opponent -1 HP.
tāu gèn (4): You +1 HP. (Can't play this when you have 禁回 debuff, or have full (4) HP.)
互傷 (2): Both -1 HP.
歧視 (2): Give your opponent 歧視 debuff. (They can't draw or play any card on their next turn. The debuff will be removed when they end their turn.)
禁回 (2): Give your opponent 禁回 debuff. (Can't play this when they already have it.)
燒燬 (1): Remove all cards and debuffs from both players.
